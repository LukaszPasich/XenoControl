Ellen Louise Ripley

Lieutenant First Class | CEO

Born on January 7, 2092 at the Olympia colony on Luna, United Americas. Gained Masters in engineering from the New York Aeronautics University. Began her career as a warrant officer with Weyland-Yutani's commercial freight operations, she was assigned to the USCSS Nostromo in 2122 when it encountered a single Xenomorph unintentionally collected from the planetoid LV-426, she was the sole survivor of the encounter. In 2179 she returned to LV-426 for a rescue mission in Hadley’s Hope colony, fighting an entire Xenomorph Hive. She has been fighting Xenomorphs ever since.

Al Apone

Master Sergeant

Born in Detroit, Michigan, United Americas in 2137. Little is known about his early life or how, why and when he joined the USCM. He later achieved the rank of Master Sergeant. He was selected for the mission to Hadley's Hope 2179 due to his expertise in on-the-ground logistics and exemplary leadership abilities. Following that mission he joined XenoControl to lend his expertise in continued fight with the Alien pest.

Dwayne Hicks

Corporal

Date and place of birth classified. Corporal Dwayne Hicks was a member of the United States Colonial Marine Corps, part of 2nd Battalion Bravo Team. He was a member of the combat unit deployed to LV-426 in 2179, to investigate the sudden loss of contact with the colony of Hadley's Hope. He was subsequently involved in combating the Xenomorph infestation at the colony. Joined XenoControl in 2183.

Janette Vasquez

Private First Class

Date and place of birth classified. A criminal offender since the young age, in 2172 Vasquez enlisted in the United States Colonial Marines as part of the Service or Jail Act in order to avoid further detention. She became a Smartgun operator with USCM, part of 2nd Battalion Bravo Team and was deployed to LV-426 in 2179 to investigate the sudden loss of contact with the colony of Hadley’s Hope. Following that mission she decided without hesitation that she wanted to be a part of XenoControl.

William L. Hudson

Private First Class

Born on May 17, 2149, location not disclosed. Hudson has not been a career soldier, but he joined the Colonial Marines in in 2169 on a 10-year deployment mainly for the pension benefits received at the end of the service. He was a combat technician in the United States Colonial Marine Corps, part of 2nd Battalion Bravo Team. Also a member of the combat unit deployed to Acheron (LV-426) in 2179. Upon complition of his contract with USCM he decided to join XenoControl to continue the fight with Xenomorph pests.

Lance Bishop
model 341-B (Synthetic)
Hyperdyne Systems

Synthetic Technician

Designed and created by Weyland -Yutani. Bishop was a technician with the USCM assigned to 2nd Battalion Bravo Team. He also participated in LV-426 mission in 2179. After that mission USCM agreed to lease him to newly created XenoControl. Bishop is the primary driver of M577 Armored Personnel Carrier. He is also programmed to fly or remotely control the UD-4 "Cheyenne" Dropship.
