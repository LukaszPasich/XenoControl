Spaceship Infestation

It is very well documented that a single Xenomorph can kill an entire crew of a Europa-class size space vessel in a few days. In the famous ‘First humanity’s encounter with Xenomorph’ almost entire crew of the towing M-class vehicle USCSS Nostromo (6 people) was killed in just couple of hours.

What to do: If your spaceship has accidentally picked up an alien passenger, your best course of action is immediate evacuation. If that’s not possible then gather the crew in the central command unit with a good supply of food and weapons and seal off the doors, air vents and all tunnels leading to your location. If you can identify Xenomorph’s location on your ship, then by remotely controlling the doors try to push it to the airlock, where you can flush it into space. Colony Infestation

Whether you’re dealing with one Xenomorph or the whole Hive with the Queen, the entire colony operation is compromised and lives of everybody in it are in serious danger. Xenomorphs alone will terrorise the colony and hunt it’s every member one by one until everyone is killed. If the Queen is present, she will lay eggs and every colonist will be kidnapped to be impregnated with a new alien.

What to do: Taking prompt action at the first sign of Xenomorph infestation will give your colony best chance of survival. Collect all the weapons you can find and gather everyone in a spacious building with food and water supplies. Weld all the doors, tunnels and airducts possibly leading to your location and setup armed teams to guard each entrance. Most importantly do not delay and contact our team.
